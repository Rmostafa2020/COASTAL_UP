OVERVIEW

Coastal UP Partners is a vertically integrated private equity, development,

and asset management firm specializing in opportunistic investments in

residential assets, including single-family rentals (SFR), & multifamily

properties, as well as Hospitality assets. Leveraging extensive experience

and proactive management, we maintain a competitive edge by focusing

on value-driven, risk-mitigated investments within our core sectors.

Our mission is to build a portfolio of enduring asset that generate

strong cash flows and long-term value.


Hospitality: We invest in limited-service hotels situated in select leisure and tourist

destinations, utilizing off-market transactions to enhance cash flow and achieve capital

appreciation.

Residential: We target properties with deferred maintenance or mismanagement

in moderate to medium density areas near metropolitan cities, emabling us to increase

increase rents and optimize value. In the multifamily sector, we leverage established

relationships to source acquisition opportunities that focus on stable cash flow,

income growth, and long-term property value appreciation.
