David Z. Newman

David Z. Newman

  • Prior to founding Coastal Up Partners, David spent nearly a decade building Empire Cornerstone, where he led the acquisition, repositioning, and management of a portfolio of Brooklyn, New York, mixed-use and multi-family residential properties, generating very strong investor returns.​

  • David holds a B.S. in Finance and a B.A. in Real Estate from the University of Denver, and an M.B.A with a dual focus in Finance and Construction Management from the University of Denver’s Daniels School of Business.​

  • He has lectured on real estate and finance at Yale University, and recruits M.B.A. candidates for the University of Denver.

  • He currently serves as Treasurer of the Ocean Medicine Foundation. David is licensed as a real estate professional in New York and Colorado.
